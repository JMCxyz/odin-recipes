Hawaiian Pizza

Homemade Hawaiian pizza features a crispy, golden crust topped with tangy tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, savory ham, and sweet pineapple chunks. Baked fresh in your oven, it offers the perfect balance of salty and sweet flavors in every bite.

Ingredients

  • warm water
  • active dry yeast
  • bread flour
  • kosher salt
  • sugar

Steps

  1. Make the dough
  2. Knead
  3. Rise
  4. Punch & shape
  5. Bake
